WHAT IS FEE-BASED EDUCATION?

In addition to San Diego Continuing Education’s free public course offerings link to schedule, we offer a series of fee-based course offerings through the Education Training Institute (ETI) at a competitive rate. Anyone is eligible to apply. Some courses are offered online. Others are offered in a traditional brick and mortar, classroom-based format.

Presently, ETI offers face-to-face, in classroom, fee-based courses

Smog Control Certificate, Auto

28 hours, $450 per person includes fee for completion certificate

Osha 10

10 hours , $100 per person includes fee for completion certificate
